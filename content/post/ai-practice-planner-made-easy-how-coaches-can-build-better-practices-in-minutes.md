## **How I Use The AI Practice Planner**

  

Here’s how it worked for me this past season.

After using the **Team Evaluation Tool** to figure out where our team was strong — and where we needed to improve — I plugged everything into the **Advanced Practice Planner**. I told it what phase we were in (midseason), how long our practices were, and how many players were expected.

In seconds, I had a full practice plan tailored to exactly what we needed: time blocks, drill names, instructions, coaching points, and even optional video links.

The best part? It stayed focused on the team-level goals I had already defined. No fluff. Just a clear, structured plan.

  

  

## **What It Delivers**

  

The **AI Coaching Solutions Practice Planner** gives you:

*   A detailed schedule (warm-up, technical/tactical drills, competition, cooldown)
    
*   Step-by-step breakdowns of drills
    
*   Clear coaching points for every segment
    
*   Optional support videos
    
*   The ability to upload your own drills or build new ones from scratch
    

Whether you're a basketball, football, softball, soccer, volleyball, or multi-sport coach — this tool gives you a system that adapts to _your_ level, _your_ team, and _your_ season.

  

  

## **Why It Works**

  

AI doesn’t replace your coaching instinct.

It organizes it.

It gives you back your time.

It helps you build momentum and consistency through the season.

And when paired with the **Team Evaluation Tool**, it becomes part of a full-season strategy that grows with your team.
