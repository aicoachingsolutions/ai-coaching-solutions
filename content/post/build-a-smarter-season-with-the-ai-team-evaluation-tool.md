## **Why I Built This Tool**

  

I wanted something that could take my end-of-season notes, my stat sheets, my rotations — and give me a real breakdown of where my team stood.

  

But not just a summary. I needed:- A clear team profile- A gameplan structure for the next phase- Coaching points to focus on in practice- Culture improvements that actually stick

  

So I built it — and now I use it every time I’m trying to regroup, reload, or rebuild.

  

  

## **What the AI Team Evaluation Tool Does**

  

This tool walks you through a full-season analysis:

  

1\. **Input your sport, level, and team info** — whether you’re coaching 14U, JV, or varsity.

2\. **Upload or type in your team stats, usage, or coaching notes** — however you track your season.

3\. **Answer targeted questions about your identity, strengths, and weaknesses**.

  

Then it generates

\- A complete team profile

\- Offensive/defensive strategy suggestions

\- Positional adjustments and communication tips

\- A seasonal breakdown (summer, preseason, midseason, postseason)

\- Practice Planner copy/paste snippets to feed into the Advanced Practice Planner

  

  

## **What Makes This Different**

  

This isn’t some generic template.

  

It responds to _your_ team’s style, _your_ numbers, and _your_ philosophy. You can upload a spreadsheet or just write in what you know. It adapts either way.

  

And when you finish your with your team evaluation, you can export the plan as a Word doc, PDF, or text — ready to build off of in the Practice Planner or share with your staff.

Before you build a practice plan, run a Team Evaluation.

\- Build real positional competition

\- Prepare systems before the season even starts
