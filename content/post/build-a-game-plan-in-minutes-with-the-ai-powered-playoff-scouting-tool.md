### 📊 **What the Playoff Scouting Tool Does**

The tool helps coaches:

*   Break down playoff opponents by seed, play style, or recent form
    
*   Generate a game plan based on known tendencies, size, strengths, and gaps
    
*   Prepare players with simplified, targeted keys for execution
    

It’s like having an assistant coach who never sleeps—and never misses the details.

  

  

### 🧠 **How It Works**

You enter basic info:

*   Opponent’s seed and record
    
*   Key player strengths (if known)
    
*   Your team’s style, strengths, and preferred tempo
    

Then the AI generates a complete report, including:

*   Strategic matchup focus
    
*   Press or zone usage based on their tendencies
    
*   End-of-quarter or late-game strategy tips
