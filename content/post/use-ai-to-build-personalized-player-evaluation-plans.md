## **Why I Use the AI Player Evaluation Tool**

  

This tool helps me take the feedback I already have — stats, notes, performance observations — and turn it into a real development roadmap for each athlete.

  

No more vague advice. Each player gets:

\- A clear breakdown of their strengths and weaknesses

\- A structured workout plan they can use at home, with a parent, or with a coach

\-Coaching points, verbal cues, and mindset support

\- Optional calendar layout and reflection prompts for accountability

  

  

## **What Makes This Tool Special**

  

This isn’t a one-size-fits-all system. You can:

\- Input sport, level, and position

\- Add personal coaching notes or test data

\- Choose how long workouts are, how many to create, and what kind of equipment they have access to.

  

You can even include simple objects like cones, chairs, towels, or chalk if they don’t have access to a facility.

  

It works because it thinks like a coach — not just a tech platform.

  

  

## **How It Connects to the Rest of the System**

  

After I use AI to evaluate a player, I can export their data and upload it into the **Team Evaluation Tool**.

  

That means individual feedback flows into team planning — and when I use the **Advanced Practice Planner**, I already know which players need extra reps or custom blocks.

  

It’s all connected. The Player Evaluation Tool is just the first step.

  

  

## **Why It Matters**

  

Every athlete deserves a chance to grow. This tool gives them that — and gives me the confidence that I’m not missing something.

  

I’ve used it with JV players, varsity starters, and offseason returners.
